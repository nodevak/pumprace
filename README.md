# 🏁 PUMPRACE — Pump.fun Token Battle Arena

> 10 tokens. 4 hours. One winner takes the treasury.

PUMPRACE is a community-driven Solana token racing platform. Nominate your token, earn community votes to qualify, and race against 9 other tokens to the highest market cap. The winner becomes the official PUMPRACE treasury token and their community receives the $pRACE fees.

Live at: [pumprace.up.railway.app](https://pumprace.up.railway.app)

---

## How It Works

1. **Nominate** - Submit Pump.fun token contract address. Token info is auto-fetched. Completely free, no wallet needed.

2. **Vote** - Share your nomination with your community. Anyone can vote with no login required. Hit 50 votes and your token automatically enters the race queue.

3. **Race Starts** - When 10 tokens are queued the race launches automatically. Each race lasts exactly 4 hours.

4. **Live Race Track** - Watch all 10 tokens race in real time on a live track.

5. **Rug Detection** - Any token that drops 95% or more from its starting market cap is flagged as rugged and disqualified from the race.

6. **Winner Crowned** - After 4 hours the token with the highest market cap wins. The winner is immortalized in the Hall of Fame as the official PUMPRACE Treasury Token.

7. **Treasury Distribution** — All $pRACE fees generated during the race are distributed to the winning token's community. Every payout is announced publicly with full transaction hashes for transparency.
